    Unsolicited Submissions Policy


    This is the legal text about sending me ideas. In short, it states that when you send me an idea, I can use that idea, and you consent to let me use it.
    This legal notice is needed due to “intellectual property”. Many people and companies have faced litigation because they came up with, or were already working on, an idea that was coincidentally similar to one sent in by a member of the public.
    For safety, here's my policy on that.



    Factual ideas and information for videos


    The Curious Redcoat welcomes ideas for factual videos within the current formats being produced. While efforts will be made to provide credit for ideas where appropriate, the following terms apply:

    • 1- your submissions and their contents will automatically become the property of the Curious Redcoat, without any compensation to you
      2- your submissions and their contents does not infringes any intelectual property, or copyrights of any other party
      3- the Curious Redcoat may use or redistribute the submissions and their contents for any purpose and in any way
      4- the Curious Redcoat is not liable for any infringes of any intelectial property, or copyrights, from ideas submitted
      5- there is no obligation for the Curious Redcoat to review the submission
      6- there is no obligation to keep any submissions confidential

    • Ideas for formats, fiction ideas, full scripts, etc


    The Curious Redcoat does not accept or consider unsolicited creative ideas, including but not limited to: fiction ideas or outlines, and full scripts (collectively, “works”). Please do not submit any unsolicited works in any form: they will not be read and you will not receive a reply. The sole purpose of this policy is to avoid potential misunderstandings or disputes when the Curious Redcoat's productions might seem similar to works submitted.

    If, despite the request that you not send your ideas, you still submit works, then regardless of the contents of your submission or any accompanying text, the same terms shall apply as for factual ideas.
